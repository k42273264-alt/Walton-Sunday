All That Sparkles Party Nights

Join us for a dazzling festive experience at Walton Hall Hotel & Spa! Our private and joiner Christmas parties are priced at £47.50 per person and include a sumptuous 3-course festive menu, tea & coffee station, crackers, table novelties, and a DJ with dancing until late.
