Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa

A luxurious 4-star hotel set in a 16th-century Grade II listed estate, Walton Hall and hotel offers 197 rooms, a modern spa, fine dining, and 22 event spaces across 65 acres of tranquil Warwickshire countryside, just 10 minutes from the M40.
