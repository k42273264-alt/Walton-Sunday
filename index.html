Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa

A luxurious 4-star hotel set in a 16th-century Grade II listed estate, Walton Hall and hotel offers 197 rooms, a spa, private dining, and 22 event spaces across 65 acres of tranquil Warwickshire countryside, just 10 minutes from the M40.

Accommodation

197 Beautifully Appointed Rooms

Choose from 197 beautifully appointed rooms, including 37 rooms in our historic 16th-century hall. Enjoy modern amenities, plush beds, high-speed Wi-Fi, and serene countryside views.

True Love Stories Start Here
