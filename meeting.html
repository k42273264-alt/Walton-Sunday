Meetings & Events

Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa, set in 65 acres of beautiful landscaped grounds, offers 22 versatile venues for corporate and private events. This historic 16th-century estate, once home to the Mordaunt family, combines timeless charm with modern facilities, including purpose-built conference spaces and the self-contained Courtyard Suite, seating up to 220 theatre-style. Our dedicated events team ensures a seamless experience for board meetings, conferences, or bespoke gatherings.
