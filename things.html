Warwickshire Adventures

Discover Warwickshire’s Charm

Nestled in 65 acres of beautiful countryside, Walton Hall Hotel & Spa by Sunday is the perfect base to explore historic Stratford-upon-Avon, majestic Warwick Castle, and the rolling Cotswolds. Enjoy cultural landmarks, scenic walks, and dog-friendly outings for an unforgettable Warwickshire escape.

Local Attractions

Nearby Experiences


Stratford-upon-Avon

Visit Shakespeare’s birthplace, historic theatres, and charming river walks, just 20 minutes from Walton Hall.

Warwick Castle

Step back in time at this medieval fortress with towers, dungeons, and live jousting shows.

The Cotswolds

Explore honey-coloured villages, rolling hills, and dog-friendly trails in this Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty.
