True Love Stories Start Here

Now you are planning your big day, Sunday Warwickshire Walton Hall Hotel & Spa is the perfect setting for your wedding. Set in 65 acres our 16th Century Hall has stunning landscapes and unique backdrop for your wedding. You can also make use of our on-site chapel, St James, for your special day. Imagine turning up to our knowing you have the photo opportunity of a lifetime.
We know that choosing the right venue is one of the biggest decisions for your wedding, so allow our experienced team to help you plan every detail perfectly.

We are proud to host all types of weddings from marriages to civil partnerships to the renewal of vows. Whether you are planning an intimate reception for your closest family and friends, or a lavish multi-day celebration with 150 guests, we are here to ensure you get hitched without a hitch.


We have three delightful rooms for you to choose from…. The exceptional Moncrieffe Suite offers all the character you can imagine from our Hall. The tall ceilings, sparkling chandeliers and fireplaces with doors that open up on to our stunning grounds create the perfect environment for your ceremony. The Mordaunt is a perfect space for more intimate weddings and private dining. The Courtyard is a modern, spacious suite which has a private entrance and available for up to 170 guests. We can also look at an outdoor ceremony with the Hall as the ideal backdrop to your photos.


Moncreiffe Suite: Licensed for 120 people for ceremonies Mordaunt Suite: Licensed for 60 people for ceremonies Courtyard Suite: Licensed for 170 people for ceremonies To ensure your dreams become a reality, our function suites and team is dedicated to making your special day as perfect and individual as you are. There are endless possibilities when choosing our function suites for your special day.


We will help you plan your perfect, bespoke wedding down to even the finest of details. We will help you organise your wedding day with the minimum of fuss and maximum enjoyment, to ensure your wedding is just how you imagined it, from your wedding breakfast to your evening reception. We offer tailor-made packages with attention to every detail, where you and your guests can enjoy a menu of seasonal, locally inspired flavours. Our Wedding Planner will get to know you, and understand what matters most to you on your wedding day. You can relax knowing every item on your to-do list is taken care of. We are also pleased to offer preferential accommodation rates for your guests, which can also be extended to prior and after your wedding for those guests wishing to take advantage of a longer stay and explore our surrounding historical towns. So why not come and meet us?


We’ll show you around the hotel, answer the many questions you’re sure to have at this exciting time and offer you our wealth of advice on everything from the most beautiful backdrops for photos to room dressing that brings your dream to life. To arrange your visit and discuss your plans with our Wedding Planner, please call 01789 842 424 or mice.waltonhall@hotelssunday.com
